About Us

We are a faith-driven home service business specializing in window cleaning and gutter cleaning, serving the great region of East Texas. Click the button below to learn more!

Our Story

Owner, Hugh Roth started this business in 2023 all the way down in Houston, Texas. When moving to Henderson to continue his studies, he brought ForeverClear with him and is now happily serving the surrounding areas. Believe it or not, the business only started because Hugh came up with a catchy name that he claims would be "a waste of creativity if not used." We have a passion for what we do, and we take pride in what we do, but the only reason we can deliver a job well done is because of the drive that Christ gives us to work hard for the glory of His name. We firmly believe that work is worship, and God deserves worship without blemish, so that is what we strive for. Some fun facts about Hugh include that he loves birds, photography, the beach, has a love for learning, and an even deeper love for our saviour Jesus Christ.
